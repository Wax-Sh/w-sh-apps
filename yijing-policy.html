Yijing Divination App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in the mobile application Yijing Divination.

1. General Information

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. Our app is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

2. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal identity information (like names, emails, or phone numbers) directly. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Google AdMob: Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob uses the Advertising ID from your device to serve personalized ads and for analytics. You can manage these settings in your device's Android "Ads" menu.

3. Data Safety & Types of Data Collected

To comply with Google Play's Data Safety section, we disclose that through third-party SDKs (AdMob), the following data may be collected:

Location: Approximate location (IP-based) for localized advertising.

Device Identifiers: Advertising ID and device info for ad frequency capping and fraud prevention.

Performance Data: Crash logs and diagnostics to improve app stability.

4. GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain data protection rights. We use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent for data processing where required by law. You may withdraw your consent at any time through the app settings.

5. CCPA/CalOPPA (California Users)

We comply with California privacy laws. We do not "sell" your personal information in the traditional sense, but we do share advertising identifiers with Google AdMob to provide you with relevant ads.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since our app does not have its own servers and does not store personal data, your privacy is protected by the standard security protocols of the Android operating system and Google’s infrastructure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
